About Me

Hello, I'm Arca Aldıkaçtı, a Geomatics Engineering student at Hacettepe University. I have a strong passion for technology, automotive innovations, and spatial data analysis.

Throughout my engineering education, I've focused on combining theoretical knowledge with practical applications. I'm continuously developing my skills in geographic information systems, remote sensing, and data analysis.

I believe that spatial data and technology have the power to transform our world, and I'm excited to be part of that transformation.
